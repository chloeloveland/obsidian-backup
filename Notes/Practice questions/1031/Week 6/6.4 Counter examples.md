**Values:** $A = \{1,2\}$, $B = \{1,2,3,4,5\}$.
**Assumptions true:** A and B are both sets.
**Conclusion false:** $A\backslash B = \{3,4,5\}$. $|A| = 2$ and $|A\backslash B| = 3$. So $|A| > |A\backslash B|$ does not hold.
